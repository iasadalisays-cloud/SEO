PPrints (UK) SEO Case Study

Detailed organic analysis showing how we improved keyword rankings and authority for custom workwear and leavers hoodies.

Client
PPrints (UK)
Industry
Custom Workwear
Target Market
United Kingdom
Website
pprints.co.uk

Project Objective

The objective of this SEO project was to improve organic keyword rankings for high-volume commercial and seasonal UK search terms related to personalised workwear, custom workwear, and leavers hoodies. The campaign also aimed to strengthen category-level authority ahead of the 2025 seasonal demand.

SEO Work Executed

1. On-Page SEO Optimisation

We focused on making the content clearly understandable for both users and search engines:

  • Optimised title tags and meta descriptions for CTR and keyword relevance
  • Improved H1, H2, and content structure on category pages
  • Internal linking enhancements to support topical authority
  • Page speed optimisation and loading fixes

Results & Impact

The campaign established a strong SEO foundation for future growth and positioned PPrints strongly for seasonal peaks.

Google Search Console Performance: Steady growth in clicks and impressions

Commercial Keywords

Significant upward movement across high-intent commercial keywords, moving from pages 5-6 into pages 2-3.

Seasonal Visibility

Gained early visibility for "Leavers Hoodies 2025" keywords, setting the stage for the peak season.

Topical Authority

Improved overall topical authority for the leavers hoodies category through structured internal linking.

Conclusion

This SEO campaign demonstrates how structured on-page optimisation, targeted authority building, and schema implementation can drive measurable keyword improvements even in competitive UK niches.
